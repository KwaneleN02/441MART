About

Life student first. Digital Art, or perhaps Media Arts student hoping to niche in Web Design, Graphic Design (specifically logo designing) and digital publication. Not really a niche considering everything that's involved, but who's judging?

So, considering that I have a niche in mind... Well, let's just pretend that I do. What exactly am I good at? Remember the first words to this section? "Life student first"! It's almost like an aha moment when you get it. But, I am good at that. I can learn just about anything, and that's my greatest talent. I like to attribute myself as a write.
